# Platos Principales (Main Dishes)

## Guisado de Chicos (Dried-corn Stew)

**Yield:** 4-6 servings  
**Temperature:** Medium, Low  
**Cooking Time:** 3-3 1/2 hours[^1]  
**Freezes Well**

### Ingredients

2 cups chicos (dried corn)  
10 cups water  
1 pound pork, cubed  
1 small onion, finely chopped  
1 clove garlic  
1/2 teaspoon oregano  
1 teaspoon salt  
4-5 red chile pods, crushed

### Steps

1. Rinse chicos thoroughly.

2. Place 5 cups of water and chicos in a large, heavy pan. Allow to stand overnight.

3. Fry pork in a medium-sized skillet at medium heat until browned. Drain. Saut√© onion in the remaining shortening.

4. Add pork, onion, seasonings, chile pods, and remaining water to chicos and simmer at low heat for 3-3 1/2 hours.

[^1]: Chicos may be cooked in a pressure cooker for 45 minutes at 15 pounds pressure.
