# Galletas (Cookies)

## Polvorones (Crumb Cookies)

**Yield:** 5 dozen  
**Temperature:** 400°F  
**Baking Time:** 15-20 minutes  
**Freezes Well**

### Ingredients

1 cup soft margarine  
1/2 cup powdered sugar  
2 1/4 cups flour  
1/4 teaspoon salt  
1 teaspoon vanilla  
Powdered sugar

### Steps

1. Cream margarine and sugar in a medium-sized mixing bowl.

2. Add flour, salt, and vanilla, and work mixture into moderately thick dough. Cover and chill dough for approximately 2 hours.

3. Shape dough into balls approximately 1 inch in diameter.

4. Bake polvorones on a greased baking sheet in a 400°F oven for 15-20 minutes, or until cookies are lightly browned.

5. Roll baked cookies in powdered sugar while still warm.
