# Platos Principales (Main Dishes)

## Tacos (Filled, Fried Tortillas)

**Yield:** 12 tacos  
**Temperature:** Medium-High, Medium  
**Total Frying Time:** Approximately 45 minutes

### Ingredients

12 Corn Tortillas[^1]  
Shortening  
1 pound ground beef  
3/4 teaspoon garlic salt  
1 medium onion, chopped  
2 tomatoes, chopped  
3 cups grated sharp cheddar cheese  
2 cups shredded lettuce  
Red or Green Chile Salsa[^1]

### Steps

1. Heat 2 inches of shortening in a heavy skillet on medium-high heat.

2. Holding a tortilla slightly open with tongs, immerse in the hot shortening and fry the bottom portion until crisp to form a shell. Fry each side of the shell until crisp. Drain on absorbent towels.

3. Fry beef in a medium-sized skillet at medium heat until browned. Drain. Season with garlic salt.

4. Layer the meat and remaining ingredients in the taco shells. Serve with red or green chile salsa.

[^1]: See recipes for Red Chile Salsa, Green Chile Salsa, and Corn Tortillas. Commercially prepared taco shells may be substituted for tortillas.
