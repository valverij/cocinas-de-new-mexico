# Ensaladas (Salads)

## Ensalada de Frijol (Pinto Patio Salad)

**Yield:** 4-6 servings

### Ingredients

2 1/2 cups cooked pinto beans  
4 hard-cooked eggs, chopped  
1 cup sharp cheddar cheese, cut into 1/2 inch cubes  
1 small onion, thinly sliced  
2 tablespoons Italian salad dressing  
1 tablespoon chile sauce  
1 teaspoon mustard  
1/4 teaspoon salt  
1/4 teaspoon pepper  
1/4 cup bacon bits (optional)

### Steps

1. Combine beans, eggs, cheese, and onion in a large bowl. Chill.

2. Combine remaining ingredients and pour over the bean mixture. Top with bacon bits and serve on lettuce cups.
