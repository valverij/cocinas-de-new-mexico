# Postres (Desserts)

## Relleno De Fruta (Fruit Filling)

**Yield:** Filling for 6-9 dozen empanaditas  
**Temperature:** Medium  
**Cooking Time:** Approximately 1 hour

### Ingredients

1 pound dried peaches or apricots  
1 cup water  
1/4 teaspoon ground coriander (optional)  
2 1/2 cups sugar  
1/2 teaspoon salt  
2 teaspoons cinnamon  
1 teaspoon nutmeg  
1 cup raisins

### Steps

1. Place dried fruit in a medium-sized saucepan and cover with water. Cook at medium heat until fruit is soft and almost mushy. Drain.
