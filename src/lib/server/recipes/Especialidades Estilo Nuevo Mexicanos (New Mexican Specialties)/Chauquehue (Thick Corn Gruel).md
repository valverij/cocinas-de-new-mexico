# Especialidades Estilo Nuevo Mexicanos (New Mexican Specialties)

## Chauquehue (Thick Corn Gruel)

**Yield:** 6-8 servings  
**Cooking Time:** Approximately 5 minutes  

### Ingredients

1 1/2 cups blue corn flour  
3/4 cup water  
5 cups boiling water  
1 1/2 teaspoons salt  
1/2 teaspoon baking soda  
1 tablespoon lard

### Steps

1. Dissolve blue corn flour in water in a medium-sized saucepan. Add boiling, salted water to mixture and cook for 3 minutes at medium heat, stirring briskly.

2. Add lard and baking soda and cook, while stirring, until thick.

**NOTE:** Chauquehue is thicker gruel than Atole. It can be served in the same manner or as a substitute for potatoes or rice.
