# Apetitos (Appetizers)

## Chorizo Empanaditas (Sausage Turnovers)

**Yield:** Approximately 2 1/2 dozen  
**Temperature:** Medium, 450°F  
**Baking Time:** 10-12 minutes  
**Freeze Well**

### Ingredients

Pastry for 9-inch, double crust pie  
5-6 ounces chorizo (Mexican Sausage)  
3 tablespoons sour cream  
2 tablespoons chopped green chile[^1]

### Steps

1. Roll pastry to a 1/8 inch thickness on a lightly floured board. Cut pastry into circles that are 3 inches in diameter. Set aside.

2. Remove casings from chorizo. Fry chorizo in a small skillet at medium heat. Drain.

3. Combine chorizo, sour cream, and chile in a small mixing bowl.

4. Place a spoonful of mixture, off center, on each pastry circle. Fold pastry in half over filling, and pinch edges together to seal. Pierce top of turnovers with tines of a fork.

5. Place empanaditas on an ungreased baking sheet and bake in a 450°F oven for 10-12 minutes or until golden.

[^1]: Varied amounts may be used.
