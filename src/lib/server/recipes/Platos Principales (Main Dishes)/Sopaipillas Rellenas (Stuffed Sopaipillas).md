# Platos Principales (Main Dishes)

## Sopaipillas Rellenas (Stuffed Sopaipillas)

**Yield:** 3-6 servings  
**Temperature:** 350°F  
**Heating Time:** 15 minutes

### Ingredients

6 4-inch square sopaipillas[^1]  
1 1/2 cups Frijoles Refritos[^1] and/or cooked ground beef  
1 medium onion, chopped  
1 1/2 cup grated sharp cheddar cheese  
2 cups Red or Green Chile Sauce[^1]  
Shredded lettuce (optional)  
Tomato wedges (optional)

### Steps

1. Cut a slit along one side of each sopaipilla with a sharp knife.

2. Fill sopaipillas with Frijoles Refritos and/or ground beef, onion, and cheese.

3. Place sopaipillas in individual dinner plates and top with chile sauce.

4. Place in 350°F oven for 15 minutes, or until cheese is melted. Garnish with lettuce and tomato wedges.

[^1]: See recipes for Sopaipillas, Frijoles Refritos, Red Chile Sauce, and Green Chile Sauce.
