# Chile Colorado (Red Chile)

## Salsa Pequin (Red Chile Relish)

**Yield:** Approximately 2 cups  
**Freezes Well**

### Ingredients

2 cups canned tomatoes, drained and chopped[^1]  
1/2 cup finely chopped onion  
1 teaspoon garlic salt  
2 tablespoons dried chile pequin, crushed[^2]

### Steps

1. Combine all ingredients in a medium-sized mixing bowl. Marinate at least 15 minutes before serving.

[^1]: Two cups chopped, fresh tomatoes may be substituted.
[^2]: Varied amounts may be used.
