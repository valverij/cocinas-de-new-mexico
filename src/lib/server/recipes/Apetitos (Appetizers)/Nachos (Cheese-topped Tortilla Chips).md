# Apetitos (Appetizers)

## Nachos (Cheese-topped Tortilla Chips)

**Yield:** 4 dozen  
**Temperature:** 450°F  
**Heating Time:** 2-3 minutes  

### Ingredients

48 Tostados[^1]
6 ounces jalapeño cheese, cut into 1-inch squares[^2]
1/2 cup sour cream
48 pieces jalapeño chile
Red Chile Powder[^1] (optional)

### Steps

1. Place tostados on baking sheets. Top each tostado with a square of cheese.

2. Spoon 1/2 teaspoon of sour cream on top of each piece of cheese. Top with a piece of chile and sprinkle with chile powder.

3. Heat in a 450°F oven for 2-3 minutes, or until the cheese melts. Serve warm.

[^1]: See recipes for Tostados and Red Chile Powder.
[^2]: Monterey Jack cheese may be substituted.
