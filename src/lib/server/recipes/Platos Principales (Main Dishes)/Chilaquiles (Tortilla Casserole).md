# Platos Principales (Main Dishes)

## Chilaquiles (Tortilla Casserole)

**Yield:** 4 servings  
**Temperature:** Medium-High, Medium, Low  
**Cooking Time:** 20-25 minutes

### Ingredients

Shortening
6 Corn Tortillas[^1]  
1/4 cup shortening  
1/2 cup chopped onion  
1 recipe Basic Red Chile Sauce[^1]  
1/2 pound Monterey Jack cheese, cubed  
3/4 cup sliced Mexican chorizo

### Steps

1. Heat 1/2 inch of shortening in a heavy pan at medium-high heat.

2. Quickly dip each tortilla into the shortening to soften. Drain on absorbent towels. Set aside.

3. Heat 1/4 cup shortening in a medium-sized skillet. Add onion to the shortening and sauté at medium heat. Drain.

4. Add chile sauce, cheese, chorizos, and tortillas to sautéed onion. Cook mixture at low heat until cheese is melted and tortillas are tender.

[^1]: See recipes for Corn Tortillas and Basic Red Chile Sauce.
