export default `# Vegetales (Vegetables)

## Papas Con Chile Verde (Green Chile Potatoes)

**Yield:** 4 servings  
**Temperature:** Medium, Low  
**Cooking Time:** 20-30 minutes

### Ingredients

2 cups potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced  
2 tablespoons shortening  
1/4 cup chopped onions  
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt  
1/2 teaspoon salt  
1/2 cup chopped green chile[^1]  
2 cups water

### Steps

1. Brown potatoes in shortening in a medium-sized skillet at medium heat. Add onions and seasonings and cook until onions are tender.

2. Add green chile and water and simmer for 15-20 minutes at low heat.

[^1]: Varied amounts may be used.
