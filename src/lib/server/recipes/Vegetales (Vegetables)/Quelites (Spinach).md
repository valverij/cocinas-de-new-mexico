# Vegetales (Vegetables)

## Quelites (Spinach)

**Yield:** 4-6 servings  
**Temperature:** Medium  
**Cooking Time:** Approximately 15 minutes

### Ingredients

1 1/2 pounds fresh spinach[^1]  
1 tablespoon shortening  
3 tablespoons chopped onion  
1/4 teaspoon crushed chile pequin[^2]  
1 teaspoon salt

### Steps

1. Wash spinach and remove stem ends.

2. Place spinach in a medium-sized saucepan and steam for 10 minutes at medium heat.

3. Drain and chop spinach. Set aside.

4. Saut√© onion in shortening in a medium-sized saucepan at medium heat.

5. Add spinach and remaining ingredients to onion and cook for an additional 5 minutes.

[^1]: Ten ounces frozen spinach may be substituted.
[^2]: Varied amounts may be used.
