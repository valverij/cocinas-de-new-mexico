# Apetitos (Appetizers)

## Jicama (Jicama Appetizer)

**Yield:** 6-8 servings

### Ingredients

1 tablespoon salt  
1/4 teaspoon Red Chile Powder[^1]  
1 lime, cut in wedges  
1-2 pounds jicama, peeled and thinly sliced

### Steps

1. Combine salt and chile powder in a small serving bowl.

2. Arrange jicama on a serving tray with the bowl of seasonings and lime wedges.

3. To eat, rub lime over jicama and dip it into seasoning.

[^1]: See recipe for Red Chile Powder. Varied amounts may be used.
