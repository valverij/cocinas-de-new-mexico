# Vegetales (Vegetables)

## Papas Con Chile Colorado (Red Chile Potatoes)

**Yield:** 4 servings  
**Temperature:** Medium, Low  
**Cooking Time:** 20-30 minutes

### Ingredients

2 cups potatoes, peeled and thinly sliced  
2 tablespoons shortening  
2 tablespoons flour  
1/2 teaspoon Red Chile Powder[^*]  
1 clove garlic, minced  
1/2 teaspoon salt  
3 1/2 cups water

### Steps

1. Brown potatoes in shortening in a medium-sized skillet at medium heat.

2. Remove potatoes from skillet. Add flour and brown slightly.

3. Mix chile, garlic, and salt with flour and add potatoes and water. Simmer for 10-15 minutes at low heat.

[^*]: See recipe for Red Chile Powder. Varied amounts may be used.
