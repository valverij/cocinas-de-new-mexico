# Bebidas (Beverages)

## Chocolate Nuevo Mexicano (New Mexico Chocolate)

**Yield:** 12 servings  
**Cooking Time:** Approximately 15 minutes  
**Temperature:** Medium-High  

### Ingredients

1/2 cup sugar  
2 tablespoons flour  
1/4 cup cocoa  
1 1/2 cups water  
1/4 teaspoon salt  
1 teaspoon cinnamon  
3/4 teaspoon cloves  
6 cups milk  
1 tablespoon vanilla

### Steps

1. Combine sugar, flour, cocoa, water, and spices in a large saucepan. Cook mixtue at medium-high for approximately 4 minutes, stirring occasionally.

2. Add milk to mixture and scald, but do not boil. Remove saucepan from heat and add vanilla.

**NOTE:** New Mexican Chololate is traditionally served with Biscochitos or dessert Sopaipillas.
