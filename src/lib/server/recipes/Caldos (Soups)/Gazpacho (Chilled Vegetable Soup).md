# Caldos (Soups)

## Gazpacho (Chilled Vegetable Soup)

**Yield:** 10-12 servings  
**Chilling Time:** 1 hour

### Ingredients

1 cup tomato juice  
2 tablespoons wine vinegar  
3 tablespoons olive oil  
1/4 teaspoon garlic salt  
1/2 teaspoon salt  
1 medium onion, finely chopped  
4 large tomatoes, peeled and finely chopped  
1 large cucumber, finely chopped  
2-3 tablespoons finely chopped green chile[^1]  
Black pepper

### Steps

1. Combine all ingredients in a medium-sized mixing bowl.

2. Chill at least 1 hour before serving.

3. To serve, pour into small lettuce-lined bowls.

[^1]: Varied amounts may be used.
