# Platos Principales (Main Dishes)

## Chile Con Carne (Chile with Meat)

**Yield:** 3-4 servings  
**Temperature:** Medium, Low  
**Cooking Time:** Approximately 30 minutes  
**Freezes Well**

### Ingredients

1/2 cup chopped onion  
1 pound ground beef  
2 cups tomato sauce[^1]  
2 cups pinto beans  
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt  
1/2 teaspoon salt  
1/4 cup Red Chile Powder[^2]  
2 cups water, approximately

### Steps

1. Fry onion and beef in a medium-sized skillet at medium heat until beef is browned. Drain.

2. Add remaining ingredients and simmer at low heat for approximately 30 minutes.

[^1]: Varied amounts may be used.
[^2]: See recipe for Red Chile Powder. Various amounts may be used.
