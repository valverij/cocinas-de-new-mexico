# Caldos (Soups)

## Caldo de Chile Nuevo Mexicano (New Mexico Chile Soup)

**Yield:** 4 servings

### Ingredients

1/2 cup chopped green chile[^1]  
1/2 cup sharp cheddar cheese, cubed  
1/2 teaspoon onion salt  
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt  
1 1/2 teaspoon salt

### Steps

1. Combine all ingredients in a blender container and process at high speed until pureed.

2. Pour ingredients into a large saucepan and heat at medium heat, stirring constantly until mixture is steaming hot and cheese is melted. Serve hot.

[^1]: Varied amounts may be used.
