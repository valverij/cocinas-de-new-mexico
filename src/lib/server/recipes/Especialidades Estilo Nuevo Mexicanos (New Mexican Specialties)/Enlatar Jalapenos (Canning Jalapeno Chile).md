# Especialidades Estilo Nuevo Mexicanos (New Mexican Specialties)

## Enlatar Jalapeños (Canning Jalapeño Chile)

**Yield:** 6 pints  
**Temperature:** High  
**Processing Time:** 15 minutes

### Ingredients

Jalapeño Chile[^1]  
6 cups vinegar  
1 1/2 cups water  
1 1/2 cups olive oil  
6 teaspoons salt  
6 teaspoons pickling spices

### Steps

1. Wash peppers. Pack tightly into sterilized, pint canning jars.

2. Combine all remaining ingredients in a large saucepan and bring mixture to boiling at high heat.

3. Pour hot mixture over peppers in each jar to within 1 inch of top of jar. Seal jars according to manufacturer's directions.

4. Process jars in a Boiling Water Bath for fifteen minutes. Test seal when cooled.

[^1]: Chile will vary in size. For this reason, there is no accurate way of judging how many it may take to obtain a given amount.
