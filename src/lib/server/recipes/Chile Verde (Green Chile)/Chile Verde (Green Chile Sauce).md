# Chile Verde (Green Chile)

## Chile Verde (Green Chile Sauce)

**Yield:** Approximately 2 cups  
**Temperature:** Medium  
**Cooking Time:** 20 minutes  
**Freezes Well**

### Ingredients

1 tablespoon shortening  
1/2 cup chopped onion  
2 tablespoons flour  
1 cup chopped green chile[^1]  
1 cup chicken broth[^2]  
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder  
3/4 teaspoon salt

### Steps

1. Heat shortening in a medium-sized skillet on medium heat.

2. Saut√© the chopped onion in shortening. Add flour and cook for 1 minute.

3. Add all remaining ingredients and simmer for 20 minutes.

[^1]: Varied amounts may be used.
[^2]: Two cups tomatoes with liquid may be substituted.
