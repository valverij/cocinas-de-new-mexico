# Postres (Desserts)

## Empanaditas al Horno (Baked Fruit Turnovers)

**Yield:** 6-9 dozen[^1]  
**Temperature:** 450°F  
**Baking Time:** 10-12 minutes  
**Freeze Well**  

### Ingredients

2 recipes pie pastry (double crust)  
Meat or Fruit Filling[^2]

### Steps

1. Roll pastry to a 1/2 inch thickness on a lightly floured board. Cut pastry into circles 2 inches in diameter.[^3]

2. Place a spoonful of filling, off center, on each pastry circle. Fold pastry in half over filling and pinch edges together to seal.

3. Place filled empanaditas on an ungreased baking sheet. Bake in a 450°F oven for 10-12 minutes, or until empanaditas are golden.

[^1]: Quantity will vary with size of empanaditas.
[^2]: See recipes for fillings above.
[^3]: Varied sizes may be made.
