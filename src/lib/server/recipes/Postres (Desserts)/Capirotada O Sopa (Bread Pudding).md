# Postres (Desserts)

## Capirotada o Sopa (Bread Pudding)

**Yield:** 6 servings  
**Temperature:** Low, 350°F  
**Baking Time:** 20-30 minutes

### Ingredients

3/4 cup sugar  
2 cups boiling water  
1/2 cup maple syrup  
6 slices toasted bread, buttered and cut into 1 inch pieces  
3/4 cup raisins  
1 cup grated sharp cheddar cheese

### Steps

1. Place sugar in a heavy pan. Stir sugar, at low heat, until it is liquified and light, golden brown.

2. Add water and stir constantly until sugar is dissolved. Add syrup and simmer for 2-3 minutes.

3. Layer bread, raisins, and cheese in a 1 1/2 quart buttered casserole dish. Pour hot syrup over mixture and top with cheese.

4. Cover dish and bake in a 350°F oven for 20-30 minutes.
