## Frijoles Pintos (Pinto Beans)

**Yield:** Approximately 4 cups  
**Cooking Time:** 4-6 hours[^1]  
**Temperature:** High, Medium, Low  
**Freezes Well**

### Ingredients

2 cups dried pinto beans  
9 cups water  
1 teaspoon salt  
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt  
2 tablespoons lard or shortening

### Steps

1. Sort pinto beans and rinse in cold water.

2. Place beans and water in a large stewing pot. Bring mixture to a boil over high heat.

3. Reduce heat to medium and cook until beans are tender.
   
5. Add seasonings and shortening to beans and simmer at low heat for an additional 30 minutes.

**Variations:** Salt pork, tomatoes, tomato sauce, catsup, or onions may be added for additional flavor. 

[^1]: Frijoles Pintos may be cooked in a pressure cooker for 45 minutes at 15 pounds pressure, seasoned, and simmered at low heat for an additional 30 minutes.
