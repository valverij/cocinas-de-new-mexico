# Apetitos (Appetizers)

## Guacamole (Avocado-chile Dip)

**Yield:** 4 servings

### Ingredients

2 large, ripe avocados, peeled and pitted  
1 tomato, minced[^1]  
1-2 green onions, minced  
Chopped green chile[^2]  
1 1/2 teaspoons lime juice  
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder  
1/2 teaspoon salt

### Steps

1. Mash avocados and mix with remaining ingredients.

2. Serve with tostados.[^3]

[^1]: **Editor's note:** Optional, can make guacamole watery
[^2]: Varied amounts may be used.
[^3]: See recipe for Tostados.
