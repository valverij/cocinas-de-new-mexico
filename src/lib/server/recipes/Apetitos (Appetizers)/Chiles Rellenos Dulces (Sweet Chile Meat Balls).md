# Apetitos (Appetizers)

## Chiles Rellenos Dulces (Sweet Chile Meat Balls)

**Yield:** Approximately 3-4 dozen  
**Temperature:** Medium-High  
**Total Frying Time:** 10-25 minutes  
**Freezes Well**

### Ingredients

Shortening  
1 egg  
2 pounds beef or pork, boiled and ground  
1 1/2 cups brown sugar  
1 cup chopped raisins  
1 1/2 teaspoons allspice  
1 teaspoon salt  
1 cup chopped green chile[^1]  
1 cup flour

### Steps

1. Heat 2-inches of shortening in a heavy pan on medium-high heat.

2. Beat egg and milk in a small mixing bowl. Set aside.

3. Combine meat, sugar, raisins, allspice, salt, and chile with 2 tablespoons of egg mixture.

4. Form mixture into 1/2-inch balls. Dip balls into remaining egg mixture and roll in flour.

5. Fry sweet chile meat balls in hot shortening until golden. Drain on absorbent towels.

[^1]: Varied amounts may be used.
