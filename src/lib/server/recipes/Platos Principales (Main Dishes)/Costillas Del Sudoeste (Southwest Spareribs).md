# Platos Principales (Main Dishes)

## Costillas del Sudoeste (Southwest Spareribs)

**Yield:** 3-4 servings  
**Temperature:** 350°F  
**Roasting Time:** Approximately 1 hour

### Ingredients

2 pounds lean pork spareribs  
4 cloves garlic, minced  
2 teaspoons salt  
1/4 teaspoon oregano  
1/8 teaspoon black pepper  
3 tablespoons olive oil  
1/4 cup red wine vinegar  
8 ounces tomato sauce  
1/4 cup minced onion  
1 cup Red Chile Sauce[^1]  
1 cup water, approximately

### Steps

1. Separate the ribs into servings and place them in an oblong baking pan.

2. Season ribs with seasonings, oil, and vinegar.

3. Allow ribs to stand at room temperature for 1 to 2 hours.

4. Combine remaining ingredients in a large measuring cup and pour over the ribs. Bake in a 350°F oven for approximately 1 hour, or until done.

[^1]: See recipe for Red Chile Sauce.
