# Platos Principales (Main Dishes)

## Carne Adovada (Marinated Pork)

**Yield:** 10 servings  
**Temperature:** Low, 350°F  
**Roasting Time:** 40-60 minutes  
**Freezes Well**

### Ingredients

4 cloves garlic  
1 tablespoon salt  
1 tablespoon oregano  
2 recipes Chile Caribe[^1]  
5 pounds lean pork steaks

### Steps

1. Add garlic, salt, and oregano to chile caribe.

2. Place pork steaks in large, glass baking dish and pour chile caribe mixture over steaks. Cover and refrigerate for 8-24 hours.

3. Place drained, marinated steaks in a 350°F oven and roast for 40-60 minutes. Serve with heated remaining chile caribe.

[^1]: See recipe for Chile Caribe.
