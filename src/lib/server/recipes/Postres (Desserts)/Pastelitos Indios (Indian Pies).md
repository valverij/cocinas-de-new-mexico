# Postres (Desserts)

## Pastelitos Indios (Indian Pies)

**Yield:** 20 servings  
**Temperature:** Medium, 400°F  
**Baking Time:** 30 minutes  
Freeze Well

### Ingredients

1/2 pound prunes  
1/2 pound dried apples  
1 1/2 cups water  
1/2 cup sugar  
1/2 teaspoon cinnamon  
1/4 teaspoon cloves  
1/8 teaspoon ginger  
1 recipe pastry for Pastelitos[^1]  
1 1/2 teaspoons sugar

### Steps

1. Place fruit in medium-sized saucepan and cover with water. Cook at medium heat until fruit is soft and almost mushy. Drain.

2. Purée in a blender, add 1/2 cup sugar and spices, and return mixture to saucepan. Cook mixture at medium heat until it is thick. Cool slightly.

3. Follow recipe for Pastelitos, steps 3 through 9, to complete Pastelitos Indios.

**NOTE:** Pastelitos Indios are traditionally thicker than Pastelitos, the Spanish version of the same recipe.

[^1]: See Pastelitos recipe above.
