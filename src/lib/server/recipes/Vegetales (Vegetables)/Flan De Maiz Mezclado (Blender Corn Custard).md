## Flan De Maiz Mezclado (Blender Corn Custard)

**Yield:** 4-6 servings  
**Temperature:** 325°F  
**Baking Time:** Approximately 1 hour

### Ingredients

2 tablespoons margarine  
3 eggs  
2 cups light cream  
2 cups whole kernel corn  
1/4 cup flour  
1 tablespoon sugar  
1 teaspoon salt  
1/2 teaspoon white pepper  
1/2 cup chopped green chile[^1]  
1/8 teaspoon garlic powder

### Steps

1. Add ingredients to blender container in order given and process on low speed. Pour into a greased 1 1/2-quart casserole dish.

2. Place casserole in a shallow pan of water and bake in a 325°F oven for approximately 1 hour, or until custard is set.

[^1]: Varied amounts may be used.
