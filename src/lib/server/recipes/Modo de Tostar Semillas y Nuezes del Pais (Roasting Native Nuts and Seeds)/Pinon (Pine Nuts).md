# Modo de Tostar Semillas y Nuezes del Pais (Roasting Native Nuts and Seeds)

## Piñon (Pine Nuts)

**Temperature:** 350°F  
**Roasting Time:** Approximately 15 minutes

### Ingredients

Piñon  
Salt (optional)  
Water (optional)

### Steps

1. Place piñon in a large container. Add salt and water, stir, and allow mixture to stand for 8-10 hours.

2. Drain water and allow piñon to dry thoroughly on absorbent towels.

3. Place piñon in a shallow baking pan.

4. Roast piñon in a 350°F oven for approximately 15 minutes, stirring frequently to prevent burning.
