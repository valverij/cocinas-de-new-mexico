# Panes (Breads)

## Pan Navajo (Navajo Fry Bread)

**Yield:** 6-8 servings  
**Temperature:** Medium-High  
**Total Frying Time:** 20-25 minutes

### Ingredients

2 cups flour  
4 teaspoons baking powder  
2/3 cup warm water, approximately  
Cornmeal or flour  
Shortening for frying

### Steps

1. Combine flour, baking powder, and salt in a large mixing bowl.

2. Add warm water to flour mixture and work into a smooth and elastic dough.

3. Divide dough into balls of desired size.[^1] On a board lightly dusted with cornmeal or flour, roll out each ball of dough into a 1/4-inch thick circle. Cut a hole in the center of each circle.

4. Heat 2 inches of shortening in a heavy pan at medium-high heat.

5. Fry the dough, one circle at a time, until golden on both sides, turning once. Drain on absorbent towels.

[^1]: For small servings, the ball of dough should be about the size of an egg. For larger servings, the ball of dough should be about the size of a lemon.
