# Platos Principales (Main Dishes)

## Enchiladas de Pollo en Cacerola (Chicken Enchilada Casserole)

**Yield:** 4 servings  
**Temperature:** Medium-High, 350°F  
**Baking Time:** 25-30 minutes  
**Freezes Well**

### Ingredients

10 ounces mushroom soup  
1/3 cup milk  
1 1/2 cups shredded chicken[^1]  
1/2 teaspoon salt  
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt  
1/4 cup chopped green chile[^2]  
1/2 cup chopped onion  
Shortening  
12 corn tortillas[^3]

### Steps

1. Combine first seven ingredients in a medium-sized mixing bowl.

2. Heat 1/2 inch of shortening in a heavy pan at medium-high heat.

3. Quickly dip each tortilla into the shortening to soften. Drain on absorbent towels.

4. Alternate ingredients in a greased, 2-quart casserole dish, beginning with a tortilla.

5. Cover casserole dish and bake in a 350°F oven for 25-30 minutes.

[^1]: Twelve ounces of canned, drained chicken may be substituted.
[^2]: Varied amounts may be used.
[^3]: See recipe for Corn Tortillas.
