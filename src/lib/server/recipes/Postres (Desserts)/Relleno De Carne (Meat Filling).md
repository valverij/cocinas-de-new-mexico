# Postres (Desserts)

## Relleno de Carne (Meat Filling)

**Yield:** Filling for 6-9 dozen empanaditas  
**Temperature:** Medium  
**Cooking Time:** Approximately 1 hour

### Ingredients

1 1/2 pounds meat (beef, pork or beef tongue)  
Water  
1 cup raisins  
1 cup applesauce  
3/4 cup sugar  
1/2 cup dark corn syrup or light molasses  
1-4 teaspoons cloves  
1 teaspoon cinnamon  
1 teaspoon salt  
4 cups pi√±on nut meats[^1]  
1/4 cup sherry wine[^2]

### Steps

1. Place meat in a medium-sized saucepan and cover with water. Cook at medium heat until meat is tender, approximately 1 hour.

2. Grind meat after it has cooled.

3. Place ground meat in a large mixing bowl and add all remaining ingredients. Mix well. (The filling should be thick and moist.)

[^1]: Other types of nuts may be substituted.
[^2]: Additional wine may be added.
