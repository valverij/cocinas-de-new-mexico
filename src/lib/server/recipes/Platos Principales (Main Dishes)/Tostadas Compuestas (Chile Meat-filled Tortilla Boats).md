# Platos Principales (Main Dishes)

## Tostadas Compuestas (Chile, Meat-filled Tortilla Boats)

**Yield:** 3-6 servings  
**Temperature:** Medium-High  
**Total Frying Time:** Approximately 5-10 minutes

### Ingredients

Shortening  
6 Corn Tortillas[^1]  
3 cups Chile Con Carne[^1]  
1/2 cup chopped green onions  
2 cups shredded lettuce  
2 tomatoes, chopped  
1 1/2 cups grated sharp cheddar cheese

### Steps

1. Heat 4 inches of shortening in a heavy pan on medium-high heat.

2. Fry tortillas in hot shortening until crisp, holding down in the center with round wooden roller, ladle, or similar object. (Tortilla will form into a cup shape.) Drain on absorbent towels.

3. Fill each tostada with chile con carne and top with onions, lettuce, tomatoes, and cheese.

[^1]: See recipes for Corn Tortillas and Chile Con Carne.
