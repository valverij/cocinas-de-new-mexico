# Postres (Desserts)

## Arroz Dulce (Rice Pudding)

**Yield:** 10-12 servings  
**Temperature:** High, Low  
**Baking Time:** Approximately 25 minutes

### Ingredients

1 cup rice  
2 cups water  
1 teaspoon salt  
2 cups evaporated milk  
3/4 cups sugar  
1 cup raisins  
3 eggs, separated  
3/4 teaspoons vanilla  
1/4 teaspoon cinnamon  
1/4 teaspoon nutmeg (optional)

### Steps

1. Combine the rice, water, and salt in a large saucepan. Bring the water to a boil at high heat. Cover saucepan.

2. Reduce heat to low and continue to cook for 12-15 minutes, or until the water is absorbed.

3. Combine milk, sugar and egg yolks and add to rice. Add raisins, vanilla, and cinnamon, and simmer for 5 minutes. Remove from heat.

4. Beat the egg whites until stiff and fold into the rice. Chill and garnish with nutmeg before serving.
