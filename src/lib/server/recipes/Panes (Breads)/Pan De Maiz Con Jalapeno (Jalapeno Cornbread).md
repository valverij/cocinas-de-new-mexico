# Panes (Breads)

## Pan de Maiz con Jalapeño (Jalapeño Cornbread)

**Yield:** 16 one-inch squares  
**Temperature:** 425°F  
**Baking Time:** 35-40 minutes  
**Freezes Well**

### Ingredients

1 cup flour  
1/4 cup sugar  
1 tablespoon baking powder  
1 teaspoon salt  
1/4 teaspoon garlic powder  
1 cup yellow cornmeal  
2 eggs  
1 cup milk  
1/4 cup shortening  
8 ounces cream-style corn  
2 tablespoons chopped jalapeño chile[^1]

### Steps

1. Combine first six ingredients in a medium-sized mixing bowl.

2. Add eggs, milk, and shortening to flour mixture and beat until smooth. Add corn and pepper and blend well.

3. Pour mixture into a greased, 8-inch baking pan and bake in a 425°F oven for 35-40 minutes, or until cornbread is golden brown.

[^1]: Varied amounts may be used. Two tablespoons of chopped green chile may be substituted.
