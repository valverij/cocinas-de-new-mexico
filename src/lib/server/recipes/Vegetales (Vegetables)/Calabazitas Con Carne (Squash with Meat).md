## Calabazitas Con Carne (Squash with Meat)

**Yield:** 8 servings  
**Temperature:** Medium-High, Medium, Low  
**Cooking Time:** Approximately 1 1/2 hours  
**Freezes Well**

### Ingredients

1 1/2 pounds beef steak, cubed  
2 tablespoons shortening  
1/2 cup water, approximately  
5 medium zucchini squash, diced  
1 medium onion, sliced and separated into rings  
2 tablespoons shortening  
2 cups whole kernel corn  
1/2 cup chopped green chile[^1]  
2 teaspoons garlic salt  
1 teaspoon salt  
1 cup grated Monterey Jack cheese

### Steps

1. Brown beef in shortening in a large skillet at medium-high heat. Reduce heat and add water to beef. Cover and simmer at low heat until tender. Add more water if necessary.

2. Add remaining ingredients, except cheese, to beef and cook at medium heat until squash is tender. Garnish with cheese before serving.

[^1]: Varied amounts may be used.
