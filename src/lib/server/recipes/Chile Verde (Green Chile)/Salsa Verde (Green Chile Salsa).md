# Chile Verde (Green Chile)

## Salsa Verde (Green Chile Salsa)

**Yield:** Approximately 2 cups  
**Freezes Well**

### Ingredients

1 large tomato, chopped  
1 small onion, chopped  
Chopped green chile[^1]  
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt

### Steps

1. Combine all ingredients in a medium-sized mixing bowl and marinate for at least 15 minutes.

[^1]: Varied amounts may be used.
