# Platos Principales (Main Dishes)

## Puerco Asado Del Rio Grande (Rio Grande Pork Roast)

**Yield:** 8-10 servings  
**Temperature:** Low, 325°F  
**Roasting Time:** 35-40 minutes per pound  
**Freezes Well**

### Ingredients

3/4 pound boneless pork roast  
1/2 teaspoon chile powder  
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder  
1/2 teaspoon salt  
1 cup apple jelly  
1 cup catsup  
2 teaspoons chile powder  
1 tablespoon vinegar  
1 1/2 cups crushed corn chips

### Steps

1. Season roast.

2. Roast pork in an uncovered roasting pan in a 325°F oven for 1 hour.

3. Combine apple jelly, catsup, chile powder, and vinegar in a small saucepan and simmer at low heat for 15 minutes.

4. Baste roast with half of baste and top with half of the corn chips. Complete roasting. (Allow 35-40 minutes roasting time for each pound of roast.)

5. Serve with remaining baste and corn chips.
