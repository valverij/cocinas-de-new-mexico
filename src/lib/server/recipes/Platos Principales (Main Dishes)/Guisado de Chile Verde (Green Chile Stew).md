# Platos Principales (Main Dishes)

## Guisado de Chile Verde (Green Chile Stew)

**Yield:** 6 servings  
**Temperature:** Medium, Low  
**Cooking Time:** Approximately 1 hour  
**Freezes Well**

### Ingredients

2 pounds pork or beef, cubed  
1/4 cup flour  
2 tablespoons shortening  
2 large onions, chopped  
3 cups chopped green chile[^1]  
3 cups tomatoes  
2 cups water  
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder  
2 teaspoons salt

### Steps

1. Dredge the meat in flour. Place the shortening in a heavy skillet and brown meat at medium heat. Place meat in a large stewing pot.

2. Saut√© the onions in the remaining shortening and add to stewing pot.

3. Add all remaining ingredients to stewing pot and simmer at low heat for 1 hour.

[^1]: Varied amounts may be used.
