# Chile Colorado (Red Chile)

## Chile Colorado (Basic Red Chile Sauce)

**Yield:** 2 cups  
**Temperature:** Medium, Low  
**Cooking Time:** 10-15 minutes  
**Freezes Well**

### Ingredients

2 tablespoons shortening  
2 tablespoons flour  
1/4 - 3/4 cup Red Chile Powder[^1]  
2 cups cold water[^2]  
3/4 teaspoon salt  
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt  
Oregano (optional)  
Comino (optional)

### Steps

1. Heat shortening in a medium saucepan on medium heat. Stir in flour and cook for 1 minute.

2. Add chile powder and cook for an additional minute.

3. Gradually add the water and stir, making sure that no lumps form.

4. Add seasonings to sauce and simmer at low heat for 10-15 minutes.

**NOTE:** One pound of cooked beef or pork, ground, cubed, or shredded, may be added.

[^1]: See Red Chile Powder recipe. Varied amounts and variety of chile will determine the degree of hotness. One to two cups Chile Caribe may be substituted.
[^2]: For a more mild flavor, tomato juice may be substituted.
