# Chile Colorado (Red Chile)

## Chile Ardiente (Fire Eater's Chile)

**Yield:** 2 cups  
**Temperature:** Medium  
**Cooking Time:** Approximately 20 minutes  
**Freezes Well**

### Ingredients

1/2 pound pork steak, cubed  
1/2 teaspoon garlic salt  
1/2 teaspoon salt  
1 tablespoon flour  
6 tablespoons Red Chile Powder[^1]  
2-3 cups cold water

### Steps

1. Brown pork in a medium-sized skillet on medium heat. Drain and add garlic salt and salt.

2. Stir in flour and cook for 1 minute. Stir in chile and cook for an additional minute.

3. Gradually add water and stir until mixture is slightly thickened. Cook for 10-15 minutes.

[^1]: See Red Chile Powder recipe. Amount and variety of chile will determine degree of heat.
