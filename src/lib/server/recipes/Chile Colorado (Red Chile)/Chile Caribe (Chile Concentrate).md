# Chile Colorado (Red Chile)

## Chile Caribe (Chile Concentrate)

**Yield:** 2 cups  
**Temperature:** High  
**Freezes Well**

### Ingredients

1-2 cups water  
8-10 toasted red chile pods[^1]

### Steps

1. Place water and chile pods in large saucepan. Heat to boiling on high heat.

2. Pour mixture into a blender container and process to a smooth consistency.

**NOTE:** Chile Caribe may be used as a base for any chile recipe.

[^1]: See Preparation of Dried Red Chile Pods recipe.
